This site serves two purposes. First and foremost as an archive of the movies I've seen in theaters since 2007. Secondly, as a data visualization of movies I see per year. All dates are directly from the movie tickets given on the day of viewing.

I started this collection of movie tickets in 2007. At the time, the tickets were keepsakes for a relationship that I was in. As the years past, I just kept putting the tickets in a black cup with the words, "Dead Man's Brew," printed on the side. Today, the cup is packed with tickets of all shapes, sizes and textures.

My current favorite ticket is from 2010 when I watched The Town in the now closed Delft theater in Marquette, MI.
